Quality Control

Quality control is done using the the very latest testing techniques and procedures as provided by internationally updated databases around the globe. The facilities have in-house labs which are completely equipped with all necessary Instrumentation and Equipment required to perform State-of-the-Art Quality Control.

All Procedures and Systems dictated by our ISO 9001:2008 are the norm and are an integral part of the SRL Quality Philosophy. All records pertaining to the life cycle of each product are stored safely and are available for Customer Audits and Certification Visits.

Manufacturing Capabilities

Junyuan Petroleum Group is a leading manufacturer of n-Hexanes and fine chemicals used for research and development in a wide span of applications. Drawing on years of production experience and the shared intellectual resources, our chemists have the skills and experience to deliver quality chemicals utilizing process routes from laboratory scale through pilot plant to commercial scale production.

Junyuan Petroleum Group offers synthesis capabilities from various locations nationally. One of our primary lab is located in Dongying in central part of China, Shandong Province, another one is located in Dushanzi, North West part of China, Xinjiang Province. Our experienced development and production teams can deliver a variety of products to meet the needs of customers in fields varying from pharmaceuticals to electronic, rubber synthesis, hydrocarbon polymerization and painting industry.

Warehousing and Logistics Services

Our highest quality n-Hexane is delivered to our customers directly from our plant based in Shandong and Xinjiang, China. We have own inland storage tanks to store 16,000 m³ capacity of n-Hexane solvents, which allow us to keep stock of abundant n-Hexane solvents in times of tight supplies. The distribution process is carried out with the use of the most appropriate means of transport, dictated by the destination of fuel, type of use and customer’s request. Safety, transport, and the production quality is our highest priority! Our n-Hexane solvents are delivered to customers in over 56 countries worldwide. Therefore the state of the art logistics channels are an extremely important factor in our operations.

Depending on the customer request and requirements, we can deliver our n-Hexane solvents via the most modern fleet of logistics services . We are also using ISO and swap body containers, drums and tanker vessels. We can also supply our n-Hexane slovents to our custom built storage sites on the customers property.
